Stitch the Cat

Stitch is a 10 year old cat. He had tumours in both of his ears and had to have his ear canals due to this. As a result of this, he is now deaf and his ears do not stick up straight (making him 10x cuter).

By us adopting him and giving him a new home, he now has a second chance at living his life to the fullest.

Many pets like Stitch also deserve a second chance. Charities like Cat Protection and the Blue Cross enable this to happen. By adopting and Rehoming these animals you can help them to enjoy life more.

Beth Mackenzie & Zoe Wicks
