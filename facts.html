Facts

  1. On average cats live for around 12 to 15 years.
  2. A group of cats is called a Clowder.
  3. There are over 500 million domestic cats in the world.
  4. Cats have sand-paper like tongues to groom themselves with.
  5. Maine Coons are the largest breed of house cats. They can weigh up to around 24 pounds.
  6. Cats use their whiskers to measure openings, indicate mood and general navigation.
  7. A cat can reach up to five times its own height per jump.
  8. The Peterbald cat has webbed paws to make it easy to grip onto things
  9. Cats have 24 more bones than humans.
